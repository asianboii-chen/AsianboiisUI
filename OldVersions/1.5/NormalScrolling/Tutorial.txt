#### Keyboard Layout ####

	[`][<][>][{][}][|][^][&][*][(][)][[][]][Bksp]
	[Tab]['][,][.][p][y][f][g][c][r][l][/][!][@ ]
	[Caps][a][o][e]<u>[i][d]<h>[t][n][s][=][Entr]
	[Shift][;][q][j][k][x][b][m][w][v][z][Shift ]

	Shifted:

	[~][1][2][3][4][5][6][7][8][9][0][%][$][Bksp]
	[Tab][:][-][+][P][Y][F][G][C][R][L][\][?][# ]
	[Caps][A][O][E]<U>[I][D]<H>[T][N][S][_][Entr]
	[Shift]["][Q][J][K][X][B][M][W][V][Z][Shift ]

Capslock also locks number row
All ctrl/alt/win hotkeys stay on Qwerty layout (Ctrl+C is still Ctrl+C, not Ctrl+I)

#### Shortcuts (on qwerty layout) ####

[Scrolllock]: Toggle this extension on/off

[Capslock]+[I,J,K,L]: Up/left/down/right arrows
[Capslock]+[U]: Left one word (Ctrl+Left)
[Capslock]+[O]: Right one word (Ctrl+Right)
[Capslock]+[M]: Home
[Capslock]+[,]: End
[Capslock]+[Backspace]: Delete

#### Features ####

Inactive window scrolling: scroll contents in any inactive window without activating it

Volume scrolling: [Shift]+scroll

Muting: [Shift]+click wheel

Brightness scrolling (might not work on some computers): [Capslock]+scroll

Window pin: turn on capslock, then [Shift]+[Ctrl]
