Features / Update Logs:

===========================================================================
	2017-DEC-16
	v1.3
===========================================================================

1. Bugs Fixed
	-   Right shift will toggle to PADV when toggled to Qwerty, even
	  other keys are pressed while right-shift is hold down. Now,
	  right-shift can be used to toggle only when no any other key is
	  pressed between down and up
	-   [Capslock+O] shortcut toggles Capslock
	-   [Space+MouseWheel] sends space after being released
	-   Natural Scrolling doesn't work after toggled to Qwerty
	-   D-Q doesn't work for right-windows key, for instance, [RWin]
	  +[R] will still output [RWin]+[P] shortcut

2. Delet-y Backspace
	press [Capslock]+[Backspace] to emulate [Delete] key (it won't
	toggle capslock)



===========================================================================
	2017-NOV-26
	v1.2
===========================================================================

1. Shifty Right Shift
	use right shift key to toggle between Qwerty and PADV

2. Bugs Fixed
	-   Ctrl/Alt/Super key will toggle to PADV when toggled to Qwerty



===========================================================================
	2017-NOV-04
	v1.1
===========================================================================

1. Crazy Capslock
	[capslock]+[i] = [up]
	[capslock]+[j] = [left]
	[capslock]+[k] = [down]
	[capslock]+[l] = [right]
	[capslock]+[u] = [home]
	[capslock]+[o] = [end]
	(these combinations won't activate/deactivate capslock afterwards)



===========================================================================
	2017-OCT-08
	v1.0
===========================================================================

1. Natural(Reversed) Scrolling [ REMOVED IN THIS S/C VERSION ]
	scroll down when up, up when down (actually pretty good since it's
the same as on phones or iPads)

2. Inactive Window Scrolling
	scroll contents in any inactive window without activating it

3. Volume Scrolling
	hold down Shift key and scroll >> change volume

4. Programming Asianboii's Dvorak Layout (PADV):
	greatest remapped keyboard, just for you--professional programmers
	(features: based on Dvorak, top row primary input=puntuations)

	[`][<][>][{][}][|][^][&][*][(][)][[][]][Bksp]
	[Tab]['][,][.][p][y][f][g][c][r][l][/][!][@ ]
	[Caps][a][o][e]<u>[i][d]<h>[t][n][s][=][Entr]
	[Shift][;][q][j][k][x][b][m][w][v][z][Shift ]

	Shifted:

	[~][1][2][3][4][5][6][7][8][9][0][%][$][Bksp]
	[Tab][:][-][+][P][Y][F][G][C][R][L][\][?][# ]
	[Caps][A][O][E]<U>[I][D]<H>[T][N][S][_][Entr]
	[Shift]["][Q][J][K][X][B][M][W][V][Z][Shift ]

5. Qwerty Layout Shortcuts (Dvorak-Qwerty):
	all keyboard shortcuts are still on Qwerty layout (such as Ctrl+C)
	(auto shift into Qwerty when either [Ctrl], [Alt], or [Super] key
	is pressed down, and shift back to PADV when release)

6. Shift-Space Bar
	hold down Space Bar >> hold down Shift Key
	([Space]+[T] inputs "T" instead of " t")     
	(hold down Space won't input multiple spaces because of this
	feature, to solve this, hold down Shift and press Space)

7. SpaceScrolling
	hold down Space Bar and scroll >> horizontal scroll

8. CapsNum Lock
	CapsLock key also locks num-row
	([CapsLock on]+[1] inputs "1")

9. Window Pin
	activate CapsLock, then press Shift+Ctrl to pin any window always on top

10. ScrollLock Toggle
	press ScrollLock key anytime to toggle this extension On/Off
